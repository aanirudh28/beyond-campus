# 14 — 50 Differentiators

Things no aptitude platform currently offers (or offers this way). Grouped;
each tagged **[phase · effort S/M/L]**. The ★ eleven are the identity of the
product — protect them in every scope cut.

## Learning engine
1. ★ **Redemption Queue** — every wrong answer returns spaced until beaten twice; "you beat the question that beat you" moments [1·M]
2. ★ **Trap-mapped distractors** — every wrong option explains the exact mistake that produces it [1·M, content discipline]
3. ★ **Apti Rating** — Elo per domain; self-calibrating difficulty; identity number [1·M]
4. ★ **Confidence calibration** — Sure/Think-so/Guessing tap + calibration curve + negative-marking skip advice [1·S]
5. **One-tap error autopsy** — concept/calc/misread/trap/time tagging driving different remedies [1·S]
6. **Solve-it-two-ways** — textbook method + genuine shortcut side by side, always [1·content]
7. **Hint ladder** — nudge → method → first step → full, with assisted-attempt honesty [1·S]
8. **Learn Cards** — completion-problem worked examples (attempt each step), fading scaffold [2·M]
9. **Maintenance probes + Rusty decay** — mastery that honestly expires and defends itself [1·M]
10. **Interleaved-by-default daily mix** with published ratios and the "why" explained [1·S]
11. **Skill-graph prerequisites** — "your P&L is weak because Percentages isn't Proficient" [2·M]
12. **Mental math gym** — 2-min speed drills (fraction↔percent, tables, squares) with PBs [2·S]
13. **Approximation trainer** — DI estimation without calculator, tolerance-scored [2·M]
14. **Misread trainer** — "underline the ask" drills for students whose error mix says so [3·M]
15. **DS decision-tree walkthrough** — data sufficiency taught as a reusable algorithm UI [2·M]

## Measurement & guidance
16. ★ **Readiness Score with levers** — per-company 0–100 + "what moves it" gradient [1·M]
17. ★ **Mock Fear Ladder** — 15-min checkpoints → company sims; measurement stakes ramp deliberately [1·M]
18. ★ **20-minute post-mock fix plan** — every mock ends in actions, never just a score [1·M]
19. **Where-your-minutes-went** — per-question mock time forensics; the 3 time-sink questions [1·S]
20. **Negative-marking counterfactual** — "skip your Guessing answers → score 61→66, above cutoff" [1·S]
21. **Speed×accuracy quadrant** → one-tap targeted remedy per zone [1·S]
22. **Same-degree percentiles** — BBA vs BBA, never vs IIT CS [1·S]
23. **Forgetting-risk list** — "these 3 skills decay this week" with 5-min top-ups [2·S]
24. **Panic mode** — "test in 7 days" honest compressed plan (coverage triage, not false hope) [2·M]
25. **Best-hour insight** — completion rate by time of day → schedule advice [2·S]
26. **Wrong-answer autopsy weekly report** — the one email worth sending [1·M]
27. **Ghost mode** — race your own past mock timing [3·M]
28. **Provisional-rating honesty** — ratings labeled until statistically stable [1·S]

## Company & test intelligence
29. ★ **Vendor decoder** — SHL/Aon/AMCAT/Mettl/Versant explained and practiced as first-class "companies" [1·content]
30. ★ **Crowdsourced pattern DB with confidence labels** — "estimated from 23 reports, Jan 2026" [2·M]
31. **Community cutoff estimator** — self-reported outcomes → cutoff bands [3·M]
32. **Placement-calendar pacing** — hiring seasons drive plan intensity, honestly [2·S]
33. **Company-filtered mastery map** — the map reshapes to show only what Deloitte tests [2·S]
34. **Submit-a-question-you-faced** — post-test reconstruction loop, contributor credit [2·S]
35. **Test-UI realism** — palette, section locks, on-screen calculator parity per vendor [2·M]
36. **Pre-mock ritual** — 30s breathing + strategy card (skip rule) before every sim [2·S]

## Habit & community
37. ★ **The 12-minute contract** — the whole product IS one bounded daily unit; home = today's set [1·core]
38. **Honest streaks** — completed sets only; freezes visible; comeback sets without guilt copy [1·S]
39. **Rating-gain leagues** — ranked by improvement, not volume; ungameable by grinding [3·M]
40. **Study circles as WhatsApp scoreboards** — accountability without in-app chat or moderation [2·M]
41. **College boards + free TPO dashboard** — college pride as a growth loop [3·M]
42. **Daily Challenge share loop** — same 5 questions, beat-my-time link (Wordle mechanic) [2·M]
43. **Learning-native referral** — rewards are streak freezes + features, never cash [2·S]

## Content & product surface
44. ★ **Business & Case Aptitude domain** — guesstimates (range-scored), case math, chart-insight, decision dilemmas; the consulting bridge nobody builds for this audience [2·content]
45. **Indian-ESL grammar canon** — the 40 real interference errors, not GMAT idiom trivia [1·content]
46. **Aptitude-to-interview bridge** — same skill re-asked as an interviewer would ("walk me through it aloud") [3·content]
47. **Real-business-data DI** — charts from actual Indian startups/economy, not "Factory A vs Factory B" [2·content]
48. **AI tutor that argues with YOUR mistake** — prompt carries the student's chosen answer + error tag, never generic re-explanation [1·S]
49. **Question pages as SEO explanations** — LeetCode-discuss long tail with layered solutions [2·M]
50. **Self-healing bank** — discrimination stats + dead-distractor detection + user flags auto-curate quality; how one founder runs 4,000 questions [2·M]
