# 13 — Differentiators

Ranked catalog of things no career platform or life-sim currently offers.
Format: `[phase · effort S/M/L]`, ★ = flagship (product identity). Shipped
items listed for completeness — they're differentiators the moment marketing
says them out loud. The idea bank for future roadmaps; nothing here is a
commitment until doc 14 phases it.

## Determinism-powered (nobody else can prove fairness)

1. ★ **Provable fate** — seed shown at start; same seed + same choices =
   same life, verifiable. `[shipped]`
2. ★ **Ghosts: the road not taken, computed** — not flavor text; a real
   replay of your seed with one choice flipped. `[shipped]`
3. **The disciplined ghost** — policy ghost that always compounds; the
   sharpest mirror in the product. `[P2 · S]` (doc 07 §1)
4. **Verified rarity** — ending percentages from real counted runs, not
   marketing numbers. `[shipped → real at N≥200, doc 08 §5]`
5. **Deck of the week** — one featured seed everyone can play; compare
   endings on a public distribution page. `[P2 · S]` (cohort machinery reused)
6. **Seed necromancy** — enter any seed number to live that world; seeds
   become shareable culture ("try 48291, chapter 4 is brutal"). `[P3 · S]`

## Multiverse / social

7. ★ **Challenge links: same luck, your calls** — head-to-head life
   comparison with zero accounts. `[shipped]`
8. **Challenge scoreboard** — "4 friends lived your life; 2 beat your net
   worth." `[P2 · M]` (doc 07 §2)
9. ★ **Cohort decks** — a classroom lives one world; TPO gets a distribution
   page. B2B2C distribution disguised as an activity. `[P2 · M]` (doc 07 §3)
10. **Ghost of a friend** — challenger's actual life beside yours. `[P2 · M]`
11. **The multiverse wall** — live aggregate of what everyone chose ("61%
    took the stable offer this week"). `[P2 · S]` (doc 07 §5)
12. **Parents play their kid's deck** — same seed, generation-gap framing;
    Diwali-dinner virality. `[P3 · S]` (pure framing over challenges)
13. **City-swap ghost** — "the life your city cost you": same choices,
    different profile prior. `[P3 · M]` (needs cross-profile replay support)

## Grounding / honesty

14. ★ **Real Indian salary bands 2026–2050** — numbers a commerce student
    recognizes. `[shipped, governed by doc 02 §3]`
15. **The fairness audit** — publish the invariant suite results (no ending
    >12%, no doomed profile) as a public page; honesty as marketing.
    `[P3 · S]`
16. **Simulated-cohort statistics as content** — "what 10,000 simulated BBA
    careers say about the MBA decision." `[P2+ · M]` (doc 10 §3)

## The report / conversion

17. ★ **The Life Report: regrets → actions at 21** — every item earned by
    the player's own run. `[shipped, v2 in doc 09 §3]`
18. **Year-one action plan PDF** — claimed report as a designed one-pager;
    forwardable artifact. `[P2 · M]`
19. **Ghost-comparison share card** — my life vs the road not taken, one
    image. `[P1 · S]` (doc 10 §4)

## Presentation

20. **AI narration that remembers your run** — scenes rewritten for
    continuity with your actual history. `[shipped, scaling in doc 05]`
21. **Hinglish narration toggle** — near-free localization via the narration
    layer. `[P3 · S]` (doc 05 §6)
22. **Season badges / Class of 20XX** — annual world refresh with topical
    events. `[P2 machinery, P3 first season]` (doc 03 §6)
23. **Ending pages as evergreen essays** — each ending a crawlable page with
    real distribution data. `[P1 · M]` (doc 10 §3)

## Clever but never (rejected on principle)

- **Real-money predictions** ("your actual expected salary") — the sim is a
  mirror, not an oracle; pretending otherwise is horoscope territory.
- **Horoscope framing** of any kind ("your career destiny") — the product's
  soul is choices-over-fate.
- **Comparing real users' actual salaries** — privacy poison and off-mission.
- **Global rich-list leaderboards** — doc 07 §4.
- **FOMO endings** (limited-time, then gone) — doc 03 §8.

## Prioritization lens

Rank by: (loop strength × litmus-rule fit) / (effort × balance risk).
Top-5 flagged into the roadmap now: #19 ghost share card (P1), #23 ending
pages (P1), #9 cohort decks (P2), #8 scoreboard (P2), #3 disciplined ghost
(P2). Everything else waits for data.
